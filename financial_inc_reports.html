Financial Inclusion Report 2018-2020

Central Bank of Jordan (CBJ) issues the first Financial Inclusion Report (2018 – 2020) that demonstrates the continuous efforts of CBJ and its partners towards promoting financial inclusion in the Kingdome during the period (2018 – 2020).

As the implementation of the National Financial Inclusion Strategy (NFIS) 2018-2020 ended; CBJ publishes this Financial Inclusion Report to shed light on the NFIS's goals achieved based on the set of pillars: Small and Medium-sized Enterprise (SME) Finance, Microfinance, and Digital Financial Services, and cross-cutting enablers: Laws, Regulations, and Instructions, Financial Consumer Protection, Financial Capabilities and Data and Research. During 2018-2020, CBJ and its partners worked collaboratively to develop an inclusive financial ecosystem, and created an enabling legislative environment wherein individuals and businesses have convenient access to and use affordable and suitable financial products and services that meet their needs, and help them improve their lives in a responsible and sustainable way.

The report shows the main financial inclusion initiatives and projects carried out by CBJ in cooperation with a wide range of partners during the period 2018-2020. Also, it shows the most prominent set of precautionary procedures and measures taken by CBJ aiming to contain the negative repercussions of the COVID-19 crisis on the local economy.

Furthermore, the report includes the Financial Inclusion Action Plan (FIAP) for 2021 in Jordan. CBJ will keep on developing and issuing the Financial Inclusion Report periodically. The Financial Inclusion Report (2018 – 2020) is published online and can be found by following the below link:
